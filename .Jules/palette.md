## 2025-05-14 - Link Accessibility and Reliability in Documentation
**Learning:** Bare URLs in Markdown are less accessible for screen reader users and can be visually disruptive. Providing descriptive link text improves the reading experience and accessibility. Additionally, direct links (avoiding redirects) reduce latency, providing a smoother transition for users.
**Action:** Always replace bare URLs with descriptive text and use direct destination URLs when identified.
