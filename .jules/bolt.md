## 2026-05-21 - Optimizing Documentation-Only Repositories
**Learning:** In repositories consisting solely of community health files (Markdown), standard code-based performance optimizations are not applicable. The "User Interface" in this context is the documentation.
**Action:** Performance optimization in documentation-only repositories involves eliminating URL redirect chains. Replacing shortened or legacy URLs with their canonical targets reduces network latency and round-trip times (RTT) for users accessing external resources.
